About The Developer

Hey! My name is Alexander and I develop and release games under the Controlled Chaos Games brand. I am currently working on games alone, but I hope to find a team in the future.

I started developing games in early February 2018. The first game was a horror browser game called "I Hope Escape". There was no final idea. I came up with the plot, locations, game mechanics on the go. In the end, I spent about 23 days on it. And ... I really liked the development process of this game, so crazy that I'm still working on it.

Like many people, I lost the motivation to make games due to the lack of results. At these moments, I was looking for something else to do: web design, graphic design, game server development, but none of these and much more attracted me. That is why you see this site, because I am continuing my work.

I have already developed some games of different genres and for different platforms. Although there are not so many of them, I tried to make each of them with my own vision of games in a particular genre. I gained experience on this and I am ready for new projects.
