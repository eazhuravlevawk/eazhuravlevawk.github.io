About SKYBOX

Skybox is a hardcore arcade game made in a low poly visual style. You should skate the cube, but don't think it's that easy. There will be no brakes on your skateboard, only gravity is driving you downhill. But don't despair! You can turn the skateboard to accurately throw the cube to the finish line, avoid obstacles, and not fall off the road. Remember! The initial throw of the cube onto the skateboard is important because it determines whether you make it to the finish line or not.

So that? Want to test your balancing skills? Do you think you can deliver at least couple of cubes to the finish line? Let's check. Download the game!

Features of the game:

- 1 location "Green Island"
- 2 modes "Classic" and "Arcade Fun"
- A wide selection of skins. Which are added in every update
- Fun and energetic music
- Challenging but enjoyable controls

Skybox receives constant updates and changes.
